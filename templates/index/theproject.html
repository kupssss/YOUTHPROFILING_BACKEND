SK Mambugan Digital Platform

Our capstone project represents a transformative initiative to modernize community engagement and streamline services for the youth of Barangay Mambugan through technology.

Capstone Project Overview

As 4th Year IT students, we developed the SK Mambugan Digital Platform to address the evolving needs of the community's youth in the digital age. Recognizing the challenges of traditional communication methods and service delivery, we created a comprehensive digital ecosystem that serves as our thesis project.

Capstone Project
