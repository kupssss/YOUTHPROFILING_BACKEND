What are the office hours of SK Mambugan?

Our office is open from Monday to Friday, 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM, and on Saturdays from 9:00 AM to 12:00 PM. We are closed on Sundays and holidays.

How can I join SK programs and activities?

You can join our programs by visiting our office during business hours, contacting us through phone or email, or following our social media pages for announcements about upcoming activities.

Who can avail of SK services?

Our services are available to all youth residents of Barangay Mambugan aged 15 to 30 years old. Some programs may have specific eligibility requirements.

How can I report technical issues with the website?

For technical issues with the website or youth profiling system, please email our technical support team at youthprofiling.thesis.message@gmail.com with details about the problem you're experiencing.

How are complaints and suggestions handled?

All complaints and suggestions are reviewed by SK officials within 3 business days. Urgent matters are prioritized. You may receive a follow-up via your provided contact information if needed.
