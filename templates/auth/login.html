Youth Management System

Youth participation plays a vital role in community development, particularly at the barangay level where local government units directly interact with their constituents. However, many barangays still rely on manual processes such as paper-based profiling, handwritten reports, and face-to-face communication, which can be inefficient, prone to errors, and difficult to maintain over time.

As the number of youth continues to grow, there is a growing need for an organized and technology-driven approach to manage youth-related information and activities. This capstone project introduces a Mobile and Web-Based Youth Management System specifically designed for Brgy. Mambugan, Antipolo City.

The system aims to digitize and centralize youth data management, allowing barangay officials to easily register, profile, and monitor young residents aged 15–30. It also enables efficient dissemination of announcements, tracking of event participation, and generation of reports for better decision-making and planning.
