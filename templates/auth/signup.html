By accessing and using this KK Profiling website, you agree to provide accurate and truthful information as part of the mandated Katipunan ng Kabataan (KK) Profiling under Republic Act No. 10742, also known as the SK Reform Act of 2015. The information collected will be used solely for youth development planning and program implementation by the Sangguniang Kabataan and relevant government agencies. All users are expected to use this platform responsibly and for its intended lawful purpose.
